Jul 8, 2015 BGC Innovation Carnival

   On July 8th 2015, as one of the key events of BGC innovation journey to create sustainable innovative environment, the exciting Innovation Carnival was held successfully in BGC.

   From the total 188 submissions of the innovation ideas, 40 teams were selected to present their ideas during the Innovation Carnival. Each team presented their ideas within two minutes in the morning and explained their ideas further to the interested people during the afternoon booth session. Their ideas target to improve the Schlumberger digital technology in many aspects, such as efficiency, usability, etc, in order to bring breakthrough business impact. People not only were excited by the innovative ideas and atmosphere, but also enjoyed the excellent performance, ice cream, popcorn, snacks and participation gifts.
